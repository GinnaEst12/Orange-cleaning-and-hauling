What Is Orange Cleaning and Hauling?

We are a dedicated junk removal company focused on helping homeowners, businesses, and communities clear out unwanted items quickly and efficiently. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing top-notch service, ensuring your space is free of clutter without the hassle. We specialize in fast, reliable, and eco-friendly junk removal, offering transparent pricing and personalized solutions for every project, big or small. Whether you need help with a single item or a full-scale cleanout, we make the process easy, stress-free, and affordable. Our mission is to provide excellent customer service, reduce waste through recycling and donation, and make a positive impact on the environment and the communities we serve

About our services

At Orange Cleaning and Hauling, we offer comprehensive junk removal services to help you reclaim your space quickly and effortlessly.
